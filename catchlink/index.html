Leave on time for every Link.

CatchLink is an iOS Lock Screen widget and Live Activity for Sound Transit Link light rail commuters in the Seattle area.

What it does

Tell CatchLink where you're going and how long it takes you to walk to the platform. CatchLink watches the real‑time arrival feed from OneBusAway and shows a single, glanceable recommendation: leave now, or your next safe train departs at X:XX.

Why you'll like it

  • Lock Screen widget — no app to open.
  • Live Activity that rolls forward automatically as departures pass.
  • Profiles for your morning, evening, and weekend commutes.
  • Works with every Link light rail platform Sound Transit operates.
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking.
