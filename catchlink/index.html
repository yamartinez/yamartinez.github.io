Leave on time for every Link.

CatchLink is an iOS Lock Screen widget and Live Activity that tells Seattle-area commuters exactly when to step out the door — no app to open, no schedules to check.

See how it works Support

Built for the daily commute.

A glanceable recommendation on your Lock Screen. A Live Activity that rolls forward as departures pass. That's it.

Lock Screen widget

A single, calm line on your Lock Screen tells you when to leave for your next catchable train.

Live Activity

Pin your commute to the Dynamic Island. CatchLink rolls forward automatically as trains depart.

Smart profiles

Different commute for mornings, evenings, and weekends? Profiles activate automatically by day and time.

Every Link platform

Works with the 1 Line, 2 Line, and T Line — every Sound Transit Link platform in the OneBusAway feed.

No accounts, no setup

No sign-in, no API key, no fiddling. Install, pick your stations, and you're done.

Private by design

No analytics. No ads. No third-party SDKs. Your profiles never leave your device.
